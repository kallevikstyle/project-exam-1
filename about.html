About SpaceX Launch Schedule

Purpose of the site

The purpose of this site is to raise awareness of the space activity that is going on, and has been going on since SpaceX was founded in 2002. The goal is to provide simple and factual information about space launches and rockets to anyone who is interested in space technology or world news in general. The details provided on this site will be easy to understand for anyone, but links to videos and more detailed information are also provided for those who want more.

This site was created for a project exam assignment at Noroff front-end development course, 1st year.
